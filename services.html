Our Services

Comprehensive home inspection services tailored to your needs

Quality Home Inspection

Our comprehensive home inspection is a thorough visual examination of your property, from foundation to roof.

What to Expect:

  • 3.5 to 5 hour detailed inspection
  • 24-hour report delivery
  • Immediate review of findings
  • Foundation to roof coverage
  • Detailed written report with photos

Note: This is a visual inspection only. We cannot legally disassemble any components of the property.

Pre-Listing Inspection

Prepare your home for sale with our pre-listing inspection service.

Benefits:

  • Identify issues before listing
  • 3.5 to 5 hour comprehensive inspection
  • Better negotiation position
  • Address concerns proactively
  • Peace of mind for sellers

Perfect for both independent sellers and those working with realtors.

Radon Testing

Protect your family with our certified radon testing service.

Service Details:

  • 48-72 hour test duration
  • Accurate to 0.1 pCi/L
  • EPA standards compliance
  • Can be combined with home inspection
  • Detailed test results

Important: The EPA considers radon levels above 4 pCi/L dangerous. Any detectable amount should be addressed.

Pool/Spa Inspection

Comprehensive 79-point inspection for homes with pools and spas.

Inspection Coverage:

  • 45-minute thorough inspection
  • 79-point detailed checklist
  • Safety compliance check
  • Equipment evaluation
  • Can be added to home inspection

Ensure your pool or spa is safe and properly maintained.
