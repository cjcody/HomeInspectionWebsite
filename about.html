About Us

Your trusted home inspection experts in West-Central Wisconsin

Meet Steve Jennings

As a former Construction teacher and active UW-Stout Professor, I bring extensive knowledge and expertise to every inspection. Based in Menomonie, Wisconsin, I serve the Northwest Region of Wisconsin with dedication and professionalism.

My background in construction and education gives me a unique perspective on home inspections. I understand not just what to look for, but how to explain findings in a way that helps you make informed decisions about your property.

Our Qualifications

  • Fully insured and licensed home inspector (WI License 3380-106)
  • Member of the Wisconsin Association of Home Inspectors (WAHI)
  • Former Construction teacher with hands-on experience
  • Active UW-Stout Professor
  • Extensive knowledge of local building codes and standards
  • Commitment to ongoing education and professional development

Our Mission

My mission is to enhance the home buying experience by providing comprehensive and thorough home inspections. Each inspection highlights items that require attention, along with suggestions on how to maintain your home and protect your investment.

My goal is to offer peace of mind, knowing that your house is your home, providing a safe environment for years to come. I'm excited to show you the qualities of the home you're about to purchase and help you make an informed decision.
